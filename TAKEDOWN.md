# Reporting a pack / takedown requests

If a pack listed in this catalog infringes your copyright, misuses your trademark, or is otherwise a problem, I want to know and I'll act on it promptly.

## How to report

Open a GitHub issue on this repo (or use GitHub's private vulnerability/contact options if you prefer not to post publicly), with:

- the `pack_id` (or the title and author) of the pack,
- what the problem is, and
- if it's a copyright claim: confirmation that you're the rights holder or are authorized to act for them, and enough detail to identify the infringing material.

## What I'll do

For a valid claim I'll remove the pack's entry from `community-packs.json` (which takes it out of the in-app browser) and, if I'm hosting the file, remove the file. I act on good-faith reports quickly. Where a pack is hosted by its author on their own link, I'll also remove the catalog entry so ELI stops offering it.

## Note on what packs are

Packs are plain data bundles - templates, entities, and images. They contain no executable code by design. Anyone who submits a pack confirms in their pull request that they have the right to share its contents. Removing a catalog entry only affects this listing; it doesn't reach into anyone's local copy.
